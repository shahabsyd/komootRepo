Privacy

Account privacy

Adjust the privacy settings for your profile. Changing these settings doesn’t affect your existing followers.

Do you want other users to be able to find you on komoot by searching for your username or email address?

Blocked accounts

Blocked accounts will not be able to see any of your profile details, Tours or Collections, and you won't be able to see theirs. We never notify users when you block them.

See all blocked users

Tours and Collections

Who can see your planned and completed Tours by default? You can always change the privacy settings of individual Tours manually.

Who can see any personal Collections you create on komoot by default?

Privacy Zones

Be it your home, work or a family member’s house, set up privacy zones around addresses of your choice to keep the location of these places private to you. New/adjusted privacy zones will be applied to all completed Tours (both Tours you've already completed and Tours you complete in the future. Planned Tours are unaffected).
